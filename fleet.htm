Overview ::

The IDF's fighting force is divided into several fleets.
The 1st Fleet is currently the only fleet actively recruiting new members. It is split into three branches, each with their own functions and duties, as outlined below.

 

   

:: 1st Fleet, Command Branch ::

Motto: "Nizom, Novil, Ne'haveh Dugma VeNenarze'ah"

"Innovate, Lead, Set an Example - and Win"

IDF Fleet Command units oversee each IDF Fleet - the fleet itself, Space Action Group, sub units and formations. Members are drawn from the ranks of the IDF Logistical Support and IDF Line Soliders commands. Started at the
bottom of the ladder, they slowly work their way up through the ranks over many weeks and months of fighting. Eventually, they gain command of a Space Action Group, and in time, perhaps even a fleet itself.

:: 1st Fleet, Logistics Corps ::

Motto: "Et ha-Kashe Naase Hayom, Et ha-Bilti Efshari Naase Machar"

"The hard - we shall do today, the impossible - we shall do tomorrow"

When a ship requires repair in combat, or a gun runs out of ammunition, the IDF Logistical Corps is on the scene. Its members' duties center around setting up rear area repair depots so that combat vessels can receive quick repair and supply in the middle of combat and return to the fight. The Logistical Corps also oversees the contruction of all ISC space based facilities.

:: 1st Fleet, Line Soldiers ::

Motto: "Tamid Rishonim"

"Always First"

The IDF Line Soliders are the backbone of the IDF. Flying ships including destroyers, cruisers, and battleships. They serve in roles ranging from advanced electronic warfare, to directed gunnery, to missile supression. All manner of roles can be found and applied for within the IDF. Recruits are also provided with live combat training. Join the IDF today!
     

   

:: Homeland Defense ::
:: Command Branch ::

Motto: "Mi Shemaez Menatze'ah"

"He Who Dares Wins"

IDF Homeland Defense is composed mostly of reserve soliders and miners, who in times of crisis, strap weapons onto there ships and join the lines. Homeland Defense maintains a professional officers corps however, to establish order and conduct perodic drills of unit formations. Its members are drawn from the ranks of the IDF Command.

:: Homeland Defense ::
:: Logistics Branch ::

Motto: "Hayitaron ha-Enoshi"

"Human Advantage"

IDF Homeland Logistics performs the same functions as the normal IDF
Logistical Command, with one important exception - IDF Homeland Logistics Command is the home of IDF Training personnel, and some of the reserve industrial hauling personnel. Its members are drawn from mainline IDF and IMI units.

:: IDF Special Operations ::

Motto: "Magen Ve-Lo Yira'e"

"Defender Who Shall Not Be Seen"

The IDF Special Operations command is a unique organization. When a convoy of blueprints needs to be destroyed; when supplies to the enemy require disruption, the Special Forces are called in. Their creedo is to only kill those with blood on their hands, and a second motto: "Through deception, thou shalt do war". IDF Special Forces units operate with great valor and distinction... and secrecy. They are solely drawn from the ranks of the IDF.
